Cedrus - Cedar

Cedrus libani ‘Hedgehog’

Hedgehog Cedar of Lebanon

Lush, dark-green foliage forms a dense mound on this slow-growing cedar. The prickly needles give the impression of a hedgehog. A very apt name for a very unique dwarf conifer.

Hardiness Growth Rate Esposure HxW@10yrs
Zones 5-9 Dwarf Sun 3’x1.5′

Cedrus libani ‘Green Prince’

Green Prince Cedar of Lebanon

Tiered branching has a natural bonsai aesthetic. The stout structure and dwarf growth rate make this upright tree a very desirable centerpiece for a small landscape.

Hardiness Growth Rate Esposure HxW@10yrs
Zones 5-9 Dwarf Sun 3’x1.5′

Cedrus libani ‘Blue Angel’

Blue Angel Cedar of Lebanon

A powder blue cedar of Lebanon with a narrow upright form. Branches hang or angle downward giving it interesting character.

Hardiness Growth Rate Esposure HxW@10yrs
Zones 5-9 Intermediate Sun 9’x3′

Cedrus deodara ‘Prostrate Beauty’

Prostrate Beauty Himalayan Cedar

A low-growing form of the Himalayan Cedar with dense, bluish foliage. Creates a mounding/spreading form with age.

Hardiness Growth Rate Esposure HxW@10yrs
Zones 6-9 Dwarf Sun 2.5’x4.5′

Cedrus deodara ‘Mountain Beauty’

Mountain Beauty Himalayan Cedar

A semi-dwarf cedar with light-green foliage, giving hints of yellowish and silvery tones. A unique and rather dense plant with excellent color throughout the seasons.

Hardiness Growth Rate Esposure HxW@10yrs
Zones 6-9 Dwarf Sun 4’x3′

Cedrus deodara ‘Feelin’ Blue’

Feelin’ Blue Himalayan Cedar

A low-growing, mounding to weeping cedar with blue foliage and fairly slow-growing.

Hardiness Growth Rate Esposure HxW@10yrs
Zones 6-9 Dwarf Sun 4’x3.5′

Cedrus brevifolia ‘Kenwith’

Kenwith Cyprian Cedar

Remarkably short, dark-green needles are borne on ascending, stout branches on this miniature, upright conifer. Its growth habit and foliage are something very unique, making this a delightful rock garden conifer.

Hardiness Growth Rate Esposure HxW@10yrs
Zones 6-9 Miniature Sun 2’x1.5′

Cedrus atlantica ‘Sapphire Nymph’

Sapphir Nymph Atlas Cedar

A low-growing cedar with bright silver-blue foliage that is very dense. A difficult one to propagate! Fascinating form and color make it rather desirable.

Hardiness Growth Rate Esposure HxW@10yrs
Zones 6-8 Dwarf Sun 1.5’x1.5′
