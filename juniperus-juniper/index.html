Juniperus - Juniper

Juniperus communis ‘Green Mantle’

Greenmantle Common Juniper

This thick, ground-hugging juniper forms a mantle of green foliage as it spreads horizontally. A selection from the U.K.

Hardiness Growth Rate Esposure HxW@10yrs
Zones 3-8 Dwarf Sun/Part Shade 1’x5′

Juniperus horizontalis ‘Copper Harbor’

Copper Harbor Creeping Juniper

A low-growing juniper with a bright golden color and copper coloration in the winter. A very colorful ground-covering conifer.

Hardiness Growth Rate Esposure HxW@10yrs
Zones 2-8 Dwarf Sun

Juniperus horizontalis ‘Blue Forest’

Blue Forest Creeping Juniper

Blue-green “trees” rise vertically from the spreading branches of this dwarf conifer. This characteristic gives the appearance of a miniature forest of bright blue trees–a very cool and distinctive form!

Hardiness Growth Rate Esposure HxW@10yrs
Zones 4-8 Dwarf Sun 1’x4′

Juniperus conferta ‘Sunsplash’

Sunsplash Shore Juniper

A variegated green and gold shore juniper.  This variety is prostrate with consistent variegation – looks incredible year-round and doesn’t burn!

Hardiness Growth Rate Esposure HxW@10yrs
Zones 4-8 Dwarf Sun/Part Shade

Juniperus communis ‘Kalebab’

Kalebab Common Juniper

This beautiful juniper is beautiful in every season, showing beautiful yellow-green growth in spring that fades to green during summer. Winter brings a beautiful transformation to an orange-bronze color!

Hardiness Growth Rate Esposure HxW@10yrs
Zones 3-8 Intermediate Sun/Part Shade 6’X6′

Juniperus chinensis ‘Swampscott Gold’

Swampscott Gold Chinese Juniper

This spreading juniper was found by Brent Markus in winter of 2013, about a block from where his wife grew up. He spotted the bright golden branch growing on a plant in the median as he was driving by. Minimal burn and soft texture!

Hardiness Growth Rate Esposure HxW@10yrs
Zones 4-9 Dwarf Sun
