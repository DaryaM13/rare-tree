Our Customers

Garden Centers

Rare Tree Nursery's vast assortment of slow-growing and unusual small container plants are ideal for garden centers.

Wholesale Growers

With new and exciting introductions as well as tried and true varieties, Rare Tree Nursery offers both small plants for lining in the field as well as large plants to use for production.

Landscapers

Large and mature specimen plants are available to provide landscapes with an established, mature look upon installation.

Botanical Gardens & Arboreta

Some of our customers manage large acreage properties with impressive collections of older plants. The unusual species and varieties offered provide these customers with an unmatched selection. Some gardens we have supplied include Chicago Botanic Garden, Los Angeles County Arboretum, and New York Botanical Garden.

Rare Tree Nursery is wholesale only. The intent of our business is to offer plants at a fair price that enables our buyers to resell plant material. As such, our customers are business owners that can accommodate a full-size semi truck at delivery and are able to unload product with a forklift or loading dock. For retail consumer purchase, please visit our mail order website for more rare varieties: www.coniferkingdom.com
