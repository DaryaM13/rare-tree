Shipping Information

Rare Tree Nursery is wholesale only. The intent of our business is to offer plants at a fair price that enables our buyers to resell plant material. As such, our customers are business owners that can accommodate a full-size semi truck at delivery and are able to unload product with a forklift or loading dock. Customers are encouraged to arrange their own transportation, but Rare Tree can schedule upon request. We typically convey plant material floor-loaded on refrigerated semi trucks. Other accommodations can be made upon request.

For retail consumer purchase, please visit our mail order website for more rare varieties: coniferkingdom.com

For shipping inquiries, please contact us via email at info@raretreenursery.com
