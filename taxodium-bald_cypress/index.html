Taxodium - Bald Cypress

Taxodium distichum ‘Twisted Logic’

Twisted Logic Bald Cypress

Twisted branches are especially evident in winter after this deciduous tree loses its leaves. It was found as a seedling by Tom Cox in Georgia.

Hardiness Growth Rate Esposure HxW@10yrs
Zones 6-9 Large Sun 7’x4′

Taxodium distichum ‘Peve Minaret’

Peve Minaret Bald Cypress

Deciduous. A dwarf bald cypress that only grows about 4 inches a year. Small rich green tufts of foliage. Conical to broad conical form.

Hardiness Growth Rate Esposure HxW@10yrs
Zones 6-9 Intermediate Sun 4’x3′

Taxodium distichum ‘Gee Wiz’

Gee Wiz Bald cypress

Gee Wiz was found as a witch’s broom by Gary Gee at Hidden Lake Gardens. A slow, fairly globose variety with nice feathery foliage.

Hardiness Growth Rate Esposure HxW@10yrs
Zones 6-9 Dwarf Sun 3.5’x4′

Taxodium distichum ‘Crazy Horse’

Crazy Horse Bald Cypress

This uncommon, deciduous conifer has feathery foliage like that of a juniper’s juvenile growth. A real curious variety with a unique texture.

Hardiness Growth Rate Esposure HxW@10yrs
Zones 6-9 Intermediate Sun/Part Shade
